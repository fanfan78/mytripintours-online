Welcome to Tours

Tours is a wonderful city classified in UNESCO World Heritage as cultural landscape. It is a witness of History with 2000year old remains and the city considered as starting point of French unity. During the 15th century, many kings used to spend time in “Touraine”, which explain the quantity of awesome castles built in the region well known as “les Chateaux de la Loire”. In the hearth of Tours, the old city kept the charm of this century with the “Place Plumereau”.

We can receive you in this beautiful neighborhood in a 15th century personal mansion named “ancien fief du Bourg Saint Père”.

You want to have a week-end or spend a week or more in Tours for holidays or business? We propose you a charming 2 bedroom flat, with all modern equipment and close to all commodities and public transport.
